What is the Rent Basketball Arcade Game?

You might have played this game in an arcade or at an amusement park. The hire electronic basketball game machine is set up on a long rectangular table, with the back to a wall.

The game has the same set of basketball hoops, but they’re side-by-side so you can compete against a competitor at the same time.

The basketball court is sloped upwards, so once your ball goes through the hoop (or you miss the shot), the ball slides back down to the bottom once again.

How Does the Game Keep Score?

Yes, the basketball tabletop game does keep score for you, so you don’t have to worry about it.

There is an electronic function on the hire basketball arcade game that also records the scores of when the basketball successfully goes through the hoop(s).

If you and your partner are having too much fun, you may lose track, but the game tracks the score for you, so the two players can have fun and not worry about writing their scores down onto paper.
