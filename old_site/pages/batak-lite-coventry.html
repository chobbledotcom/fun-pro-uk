What Is Batak Lite?

Batak Lite is a compact and portable version of Batak Pro, offering an engaging and energetic activity that people of all ages can enjoy. Featuring a series of illuminated targets, the game is designed to challenge reflexes, improve coordination, and enhance reaction speeds.

Interactive games like Batak Lite are a great choice for occasions such as conferences, team building days, promotional events, and more. They provide an entertaining and competitive environment, making them an excellent icebreaker.
