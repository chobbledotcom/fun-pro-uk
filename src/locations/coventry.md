      ## **Planning game hire for a Coventry event**


      There are a few practical considerations worth thinking through before you
      confirm your game choices.


      Guest numbers and floor space matter more than many people realise. Games
      need enough clear space to operate safely, and you'll want room for a
      small queue to form without blocking walkways or fire exits. If you share
      your floor plan with us early, we can help you work out what fits
      comfortably.


      Your audience mix is equally important. A group that spans different ages,
      physical abilities and personality types needs a range of options rather
      than a single high-energy game that only appeals to a subset of your
      guests. Think about who might feel left out if the entertainment skews too
      competitive or too physical, and consider whether a mix of active games
      and lower-key options would serve the group better.


      Your event objectives should guide your choices too. ++[Exhibition
      stands](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/events/exhibition-games/)++ need games
      that draw footfall and start conversations quickly. Team building days
      need activities that sustain engagement across several hours and encourage
      people to interact with colleagues they don't usually spend time with.
      Evening receptions need something conversational that doesn't dominate the
      room. Each calls for a different approach.


      On logistics, most games need a standard mains socket and a clear floor
      area of around two to three metres. If your venue has unusual constraints,
      let us know and we can suggest alternatives that work within those limits.


      One genuine advantage of being Coventry-based: for local events,
      last-minute additions or schedule changes are often workable. If something
      shifts in the week before your event, we're usually able to accommodate it
      because we're just down the road. That kind of flexibility is harder to
      offer when delivery involves a four-hour drive.


      If branding is part of your brief, raise it at the quoting stage.
      Custom-wrapped games are one of the more effective ways to reinforce your
      company's presence at an exhibition or activation, and because we handle
      design and print in-house, you're dealing with one team from start to
      finish. Lead time is needed, so the sooner you raise it, the better.
