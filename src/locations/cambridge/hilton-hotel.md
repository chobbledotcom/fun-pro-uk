# **Game Hire at Hilton Cambridge City Centre**

If you've booked an event at the Hilton Cambridge City Centre and you're thinking about how to keep your guests engaged between sessions, on an ++[exhibition stand](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/events/exhibition-games/)++, or throughout the evening, we can help. Fun Pro supplies ++[interactive games](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/interactive-game-hire/)++, ++[arcade machines](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/arcade-games/)++, and event entertainment to events taking place at the Hilton on Downing Street. 

We don't hire out the venue itself; that side is handled by the Hilton's own events team. What we do is ++[deliver](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/locations/)++, set up, and collect the entertainment that fills the room you've booked, so the guest experience is one less thing on your list.

We've been supplying corporate events across the UK since 2009, and ++[our team](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/team/)++ is based around 90 minutes away in Coventry. From that central Midlands base we regularly bring games across to Cambridge along the A14, serving hotel venues, conference suites, and college events throughout the city.

## **About the Hilton Cambridge City Centre as an Events Venue**

The Hilton sits on Downing Street in the heart of Cambridge, a short walk from King's College, Corpus Christi College, and the Fitzwilliam Museum, and around 20 minutes on foot from Cambridge railway station. On-site parking is available, which is worth knowing in a city where central spaces are limited.

For event planners, the useful figures are the function rooms. The hotel has five pillar-free meeting suites along with a central atrium, built-in AV in each room, and around 316 square metres of total event space. The largest room, the Isaac Newton Suite, holds up to 220 guests theatre-style and can be divided into two, while the smaller suites suit boardroom meetings and breakout sessions. The whole space can be booked exclusively, with a combined capacity of roughly 670 across all rooms and the atrium. Venue bookings are made directly through the Hilton's events team.

## **Function Rooms at the Hilton and the Games That Suit Them**

Different spaces work well for different kinds of entertainment. Once you know which room your event is in, we can recommend games that fit the footprint, power setup, and style of the occasion.

### **The Isaac Newton Suite**

This is the hotel's largest space, holding up to 220 delegates theatre-style and divided into two smaller rooms, with its own separate entrance and private bar. That makes it useful for conferences with breakout sessions or evening events that need a mix of zones. With this much floor area to play with, larger-footprint games work well. Think ++[racing car simulators](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/racing-car-simulators/)++, ++[Batak Pro](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/batak-pro/)++ reaction games, prize cranes, and ++[photo booths](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/classic-photo-booths/)++ for post-dinner entertainment.

### **The Atrium**

The light-filled central atrium can be hired alongside the suites or as a standalone space for receptions and exhibitions. Its open plan makes it well suited to footfall-driving games that pull people together as they arrive or move between sessions. ++[Grab A Grand](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/grab-a-grand/)++ cash cubes, ++[prize wheels](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/prize-wheel/)++, and retro ++[arcade machines](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/arcade-games/)++ all work well here, particularly for drinks receptions or brand activations where you want something that draws a crowd. 

### **The Hawking, Byron, Tennyson and Wordsworth Suites**

These smaller suites suit gatherings of roughly 15 to 60 guests, depending on the room and layout. Space is more limited here, so compact games work best. ++[Shuffleboard](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/shuffleboard-hire/)++ and other ++[pub games](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/pub-games/)++, smaller retro arcades, and prize wheels all fit neatly without dominating the room. They work well for breakout entertainment during a conference day, or as a relaxed zone alongside a meeting in the Isaac Newton Suite.

## **Planning Entertainment for a Hilton Cambridge Event**

A few practical things are worth thinking about early. Once the Hilton has confirmed your room, it's helpful to share the floor plan with us. That way we can advise on which games fit comfortably, where power points sit relative to your layout, and how to keep walkways clear for guest flow.

Access and loading matter too, particularly for a city-centre hotel. Downing Street sits in central Cambridge, so it's worth checking with your events contact at the Hilton about delivery access and timing; we'll work around whatever they confirm.

In terms of lead time, two to four weeks' notice is usually comfortable for most games. If your event falls in November or December, we'd recommend booking earlier, as those dates tend to fill up first.

Because the venue handles a real mix of meetings, conferences, and celebrations, it's worth thinking about the guests attending and choosing games that everyone can join in with. We'll happily talk through game options that work for different ages, abilities, and comfort levels. You can read more about our approach on the corporate events page.
