## Cambridge Venues We Serve

Cambridge offers a distinctive mix of event spaces. The Cambridge Corn Exchange hosts everything from conferences to gala dinners. The Guildhall provides a central city venue for formal corporate occasions. University colleges open their historic halls and gardens for private events, offering settings you simply cannot replicate elsewhere. Beyond the city centre, the science and business parks around the Cambridge Biomedical Campus and Cambridge Science Park host regular corporate gatherings in more contemporary spaces.

We've delivered to venues across Cambridge and Cambridgeshire, and we understand the practical realities — navigating narrow city centre streets for load-in, working within the specific rules that college venues set, and setting up in spaces that weren't originally designed for events. These details matter, and our experience with them means fewer surprises on the day.
