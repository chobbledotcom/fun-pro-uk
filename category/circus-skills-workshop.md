# **Circus Skills Workshop Hire for Your Next Event**

## What Are Circus Skills Workshops?

Are you looking for something a little different for your next event that everyone can enjoy? Our circus skills workshops provide people of all ages with a fun and memorable experience that allows them to explore the art of circus performing.

From complete beginners to balance beam pros, these fun workshops are perfect for events like school fetes, corporate [team building days](../pages/team-building-ideas.html), festivals, and more.

### Skills You’ll Learn:

**Juggling** – Master the art of three-ball juggling and beyond

**Plate Spinning** – Test your balance and coordination

**Diabolo & Poi** – Develop dexterity with these mesmerising props

**Hula Hooping** – Learn tricks and improve core strength

**Tightrope Walking & Stilt Walking** (subject to venue suitability)

Our expert instructors are there to provide a fun, safe, and rewarding experience, helping participants build confidence, improve coordination while being introduced to circus arts.

Say goodbye to forced fun and awkward icebreakers and hello to a memorable day that everyone will want to get involved in by learning new circus techniques and skills.

### Community events and festivals

Bring the local community together with one of our circus skills workshops, helping everyone have fun with inclusive activities.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What circus skills can we learn?

You’ll learn a wide range of circus skills, including juggling, balancing, plate spinning, hula hooping, and much more.

### How long do the workshops last?

You can choose the length of your workshop to suit your needs, and typically these will last from 2 hours to 6 hours

### What is included in the workshop package?

Your circus skills workshop hire package will include set up at your chosen location, as well as the kits, games, instructors, safety training and more.

### How do I book a workshop?

You can book your workshop directly with us at Fun Pro by getting in touch with our team [here.](../pages/contact-fun-pro-uk.html) We will be happy to discuss the details of your event and provide you with a free, no obligation quote.

### What are the prices?

The prices for the circus skills workshop depend on factors such as the size of your event, the location, and what you’d like to have included.

### What age groups are the workshops suitable for?

These circus skills workshops are suitable for all ages, which makes it perfect for a wide range of events.

### What kind of space do I need?

While our circus workshops can accommodate different venue sizes, it’s best to make sure you have plenty of space before booking. A large indoor space such as a gymnasium, conference room, or outdoor space like a large lawn or field is perfect for this type of activity.
