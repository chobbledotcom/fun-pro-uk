## Planning Your Corporate Wellbeing Days:

To ensure your corporate wellbeing day is successful, consider the following steps:

1.  Choose a Date: Select a day that minimizes disruption to business operations and maximizes attendance.
    
2.  Decide on a Location: Whether it’s an outdoor space or a large indoor area, ensure it can accommodate the activities you plan to include.
    
3.  Weather Contingency Plan: Always have a backup plan in case of bad weather, especially if your event is outdoors.
    
4.  Engage Your Staff: Seek ideas and feedback from your staff, and consider extending invitations to their families if appropriate.
    
5.  Select Activities: Decide on [hire corporate wellbeing activities](/categories/corporate-entertainment/#content) that cater to various interests and promote inclusivity.
    
6.  Schedule the Day: Plan out the timing and locations of activities to ensure a smooth flow throughout the day.
    
7.  Promote Internally: Build anticipation and excitement by promoting the event through internal communication channels.
