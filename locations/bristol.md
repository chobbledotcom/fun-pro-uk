The [Batak Lite](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/batak-lite/) is a more compact option, worth considering if your floor space is limited.

      ## Bristol Venues We Know Well

      Fun Pro UK has delivered to venues across Bristol regularly over the years. That hands-on experience means we understand the practical realities of each space, from loading access and floor surfaces to power availability and timing windows.
      ### Ashton Gate Stadium

      Ashton Gate is one of the largest event spaces in the South West, with a range of function rooms, conference suites, and executive spaces available for corporate hire. The scale of the venue makes it well suited to larger corporate fun days and staff celebrations where you want room for several game stations to run simultaneously. It's worth planning the layout carefully with us in advance, as different areas of the stadium have varying floor plans and access routes.

      ### Aerospace Bristol

      Aerospace Bristol is a striking venue for corporate dinners, award evenings, and conferences, with events taking place in the Concorde Hangar and a dedicated conference suite. The more formal atmosphere of events here lends itself well to [photo booths and magic mirrors](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/photo-booths-and-magic-mirrors/) as post-dinner entertainment, and to reaction games placed in pre-dinner reception areas. The conference suite is a more contained space, so compact game options are worth discussing if your event is based there rather than in the main hangar.

      ### Paintworks Event Space

      The Paintworks is a large, blank-canvas warehouse venue in Bristol's creative quarter and one of the most flexible spaces in the city for corporate events, product launches, and brand activations. The open floor plan gives us real freedom to plan the game layout around your event flow, and the industrial setting works well with branded game setups. If you're planning an exhibition-style activation or a company event where the layout matters, Paintworks is a venue worth considering.

      ### M Shed and Harbourside Venues

      The cluster of event spaces along Bristol's Harbourside, including M Shed, are well suited to brand activations, product launches, and evening corporate events. The open, industrial character of these spaces works naturally with interactive games, and the waterfront setting makes them a popular choice for Bristol-based businesses hosting external clients or larger company gatherings.

      ### Future Inn Bristol

      Future Inn Bristol has a dedicated conference centre on the top floor with multiple meeting rooms and good natural light. The venue is a practical choice for corporate away days, training events, and internal conferences. Games placed in breakout areas between session rooms work particularly well here, giving delegates something to do in the gaps without pulling them away from the event programme. Compact options like [Roll & Bowl](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/roll-and-bowl/), [arcade machines](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/arcade-games/), and prize games are reliable choices for this kind of setting.

      ### Other Venues Across Bristol

      Fun Pro UK also delivers regularly to hotels, university venues — including the University of Bristol and UWE Bristol — office spaces, and outdoor event sites across the wider Bristol area and South West. If your venue isn't listed here, [get in touch](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/contact-fun-pro-uk/) and the team can advise on what's possible.
