# Event Hire Reading

Reading sits at the heart of the Thames Valley, one of the UK's most concentrated hubs of corporate activity. Major technology, telecoms and financial services companies are headquartered here, and they generate a constant demand for corporate entertainment — team building events, client hospitality, staff celebrations and conference entertainment. We deliver to venues across Reading, Berkshire and the wider Thames Valley corridor.

Based in Coventry, we're around 80 miles from Reading, roughly 1.5 hours down the M40. Every booking includes delivery, professional setup and collection — our team handles the logistics so you can concentrate on your event.

Our range includes [interactive games](/categories/interactive-game-hire/#content) like [Batak](/categories/batak/#content) reaction games and [racing simulators](/products/racing-car-simulators/#content), [photo booths and magic mirrors](/categories/photo-booths-and-magic-mirrors/#content), [prize games](/categories/prize-games/#content) including cash grabbers and prize wheels, and [fun food catering](/categories/fun-foods/#content) from pizza ovens to popcorn carts. For [exhibitions](/categories/branded-exhibition-games/#content) and trade shows, we offer full custom branding on games — all designed and produced in-house.

Whether you're planning a tech company team day, a conference at a Thames Valley hotel, a staff appreciation event or a [brand activation](/events/brand-activation/#content) at the Select Car Leasing Stadium, we can recommend entertainment that suits your occasion.
