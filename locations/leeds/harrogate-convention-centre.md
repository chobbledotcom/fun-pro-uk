# Event Hire at the Harrogate Convention Centre

The Harrogate Convention Centre is one of Yorkshire's premier event venues, with a 2,000-seat auditorium, 10 event halls and a range of meeting spaces. It hosts conferences, exhibitions and trade shows throughout the year.

We deliver interactive game hire to the Harrogate Convention Centre and handle setup, operation support and collection as standard on every booking.

Our [interactive games](/categories/interactive-game-hire) are designed to draw visitors to your stand. The [Batak Pro](/products/batak-pro) is a popular choice for exhibitions — contestants compete to hit flashing lights as fast as possible, and the competition draws a crowd. Run a leaderboard and offer a prize for the top score to keep people coming back throughout the day.
