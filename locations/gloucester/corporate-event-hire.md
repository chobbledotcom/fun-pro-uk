## Key Venues Across Gloucestershire

Cheltenham Racecourse is the county's flagship events venue, hosting conferences, exhibitions and corporate hospitality days alongside its racing calendar. The Centaur, its dedicated conference and events space, accommodates large-scale corporate gatherings. We've delivered to racecourse events where the atmosphere is lively and competitive, and our games fit right into that energy.

In Gloucester itself, Kingsholm Stadium offers event spaces with a sporting backdrop, while Gloucester Cathedral hosts formal dinners and receptions in one of England's finest medieval buildings. Hotels and conference centres across the city handle the day-to-day business events that keep the local corporate calendar full.

Further afield, the Cotswolds provide a steady stream of corporate away days and team retreats. Country house hotels and rural venues offer a change of scenery that companies value for off-site strategy days and reward events.
