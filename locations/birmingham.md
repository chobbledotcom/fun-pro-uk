# Event Hire Birmingham

Birmingham is one of the busiest cities in the UK for corporate events, exhibitions and conferences, and we deliver to venues across the city regularly. The [NEC](/locations/birmingham/nec/#content), [ICC](/locations/birmingham/game-hire-icc/#content) and [Hilton Metropole](/locations/birmingham/hilton-metropole/#content) are all venues we know well, alongside hotels, offices and event spaces throughout the West Midlands.

Based in Coventry, we're less than 30 minutes away. That means straightforward logistics, lower delivery costs and the ability to respond quickly if your plans change. We handle setup, operation and collection as standard on every booking.

Our range covers [interactive games](/categories/interactive-game-hire/#content) like [Batak](/categories/batak/#content) reaction games and [racing simulators](/products/racing-car-simulators/#content), [photo booths and magic mirrors](/categories/photo-booths-and-magic-mirrors/#content), [prize games](/categories/prize-games/#content) including crane grabbers and prize wheels, and [fun food catering](/categories/fun-foods/#content) from hog roasts to pizza ovens. For exhibitions and trade shows, we offer full [custom branding](/categories/branded-exhibition-games/#content) on games — designed, printed and applied in-house.

Whether you're planning a team building day, a conference ice-breaker, a staff Christmas party or a brand activation at the NEC, we can talk through what works for your event and venue.
