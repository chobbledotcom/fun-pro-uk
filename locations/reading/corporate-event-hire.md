Its conference and banqueting suites handle everything from intimate client dinners to large company celebrations. We've delivered to events at the stadium where the setting itself generates energy and excitement.

Reading's hotels and conference centres along the M4 corridor — including venues in Green Park, Thames Valley Park and Arlington Business Park — cater for the region's ongoing corporate event needs. These modern business park venues are well set up for entertainment delivery, with good access and flexible spaces.

Beyond Reading itself, the Thames Valley stretches to encompass Bracknell, Wokingham, Maidenhead and Slough, all of which have significant corporate populations. Country house hotels in the Berkshire countryside offer a more characterful alternative for away days and client entertainment.

Reading also benefits from excellent transport links. The M4 provides fast road connections, and Great Western Railway services from Reading station bring guests from London Paddington in under half an hour. For event organisers, this accessibility means higher attendance and easier logistics for guests travelling from different locations.
