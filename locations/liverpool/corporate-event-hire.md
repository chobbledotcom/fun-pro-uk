## Liverpool's Key Corporate Venues

The ACC Liverpool complex dominates the city's conference and exhibition calendar. It includes the Exhibition Centre Liverpool for trade shows and large exhibitions, the M&S Bank Arena for major events and concerts, and the ACC convention centre itself for conferences and corporate gatherings. These are world-class facilities on the waterfront, and we've delivered to events there on numerous occasions.

The Albert Dock area offers character venues — converted maritime buildings that provide a distinctive backdrop for corporate dinners, product launches and client events. Hotels across the city centre cater for conference delegate accommodation and host their own programme of corporate events in function suites and ballrooms.

Beyond the city centre, business parks and corporate offices across Merseyside host regular team building days, departmental celebrations and company milestones. These smaller events often benefit the most from well-chosen entertainment, because they're about bringing a specific group of people together.
