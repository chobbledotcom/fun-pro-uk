# Event Hire Leicester

Leicester is one of our closest major cities — we're based in Coventry, just a short drive along the M69. That proximity means lower delivery costs and the flexibility to respond quickly when event plans change.

We deliver regularly to venues across Leicester and the East Midlands, from city centre hotels and conference facilities to corporate offices and university campuses. Leicester's mix of established businesses, thriving universities and growing tech sector creates steady demand for corporate entertainment, and we've been meeting it for over 15 years.

Our hire range covers [interactive games](/categories/interactive-game-hire/#content) including [Batak](/categories/batak/#content) reaction games, [racing simulators](/products/racing-car-simulators/#content) and [whack-a-mole](/products/whack-a-mole-game-hire/#content), [photo booths and magic mirrors](/categories/photo-booths-and-magic-mirrors/#content), [prize games](/categories/prize-games/#content) such as [crane grabbers](/products/prize-crane-arcade-grabber/#content) and [plinko boards](/products/plinko-game-hire/#content), and [fun food catering](/categories/fun-foods/#content) from pizza ovens to ice cream vans. For [exhibitions and brand activations](/categories/branded-exhibition-games/#content), we handle full custom branding on games in-house — design, print and application all under one roof.

Every booking includes delivery, setup, on-site support and collection as standard, regardless of the event type.
