# Event Hire Leeds

Leeds is Yorkshire's commercial centre and hosts a busy calendar of corporate events, conferences and exhibitions throughout the year. We deliver entertainment hire to venues across the city and the wider region, from the city centre's conference hotels to business parks and corporate offices.

We're based in Coventry, with direct access up the M1 to Leeds. Every booking includes delivery, professional setup and collection — our team arrives ahead of your event, gets everything running and stays to support as needed.

Our range includes [interactive games](/categories/interactive-game-hire/#content) like [Batak](/categories/batak/#content) reaction games, [racing simulators](/products/racing-car-simulators/#content) and [mega wire](/products/giant-buzz-wire/#content), alongside [photo booths and magic mirrors](/categories/photo-booths-and-magic-mirrors/#content), [prize games](/categories/prize-games/#content) including [cash grabbers](/products/cash-grabber-machine-hire/#content) and [prize wheels](/products/prize-wheel/#content), and [fun food catering](/categories/fun-foods/#content) from hog roasts to candy floss. For trade shows and [exhibition stands](/categories/branded-exhibition-games/#content), we offer full custom branding on games — all designed and produced in-house.

Whether you're organising a [corporate event](/categories/corporate-entertainment/#content), team building day, awards dinner or brand activation, we can recommend entertainment that suits your venue, audience and objectives.
