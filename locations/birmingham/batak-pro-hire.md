## What is Batak Pro?

Batak Pro is the gold standard in reaction testing games, beloved by people of all ages. Players must hit illuminated targets as they randomly light up within a 60-second timeframe. The faster your reactions, the higher your score.

This addictively simple concept creates natural and healthy competition as participants try to beat their colleagues' scores or claim the top spot on the leaderboard. The sleek, professional design makes it a perfect centrepiece for:

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much space does the Batak Pro require?

The unit itself measures 2m x 1.7m x 0.9m, but we recommend allowing at least 3m x 2m space around the unit so that there’s plenty of space for anyone who’s playing as well as those who want to watch.

### Can the Batak Pro be used outdoors?

Due to the game requiring a source of electricity, Batak Pro can only be used indoors.

### Do you provide staff to operate the Batak Pro game?

Yes, all our Batak Pro hire packages include a professional operator who will manage the game, explain the rules, and encourage participation.
