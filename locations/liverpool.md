# Event Hire Liverpool

Liverpool is one of the UK's major conference and exhibition destinations, with a waterfront events district that attracts large-scale corporate gatherings year-round. We deliver entertainment hire to venues across the city and wider Merseyside, from the ACC Liverpool complex on the waterfront to hotels, offices and event spaces throughout the region.

Based in Coventry, we're around 100 miles away — roughly two hours up the M6. It's a route our team travels regularly for events at Liverpool's busy conference venues. Every booking includes delivery, professional setup and collection as standard.

Our range includes [interactive games](/categories/interactive-game-hire/#content) like [Batak](/categories/batak/#content) reaction games and [racing simulators](/products/racing-car-simulators/#content), [photo booths and magic mirrors](/categories/photo-booths-and-magic-mirrors/#content), [prize games](/categories/prize-games/#content) including cash grabbers and prize wheels, and [fun food catering](/categories/fun-foods/#content) from hog roasts to pizza ovens. For Liverpool's thriving exhibition and trade show circuit, we provide full [custom branding](/categories/branded-exhibition-games/#content) on games — designed, printed and applied in-house.

Whether you're exhibiting at the Exhibition Centre Liverpool, hosting a gala dinner on the waterfront, running a team building event or planning a [brand activation](/events/brand-activation/#content), we can recommend entertainment that fits your event and venue.
