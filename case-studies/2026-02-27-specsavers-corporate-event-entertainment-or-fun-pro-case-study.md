# **Specsavers Case Study**

![](/images/fun-pro-specsavers.jpg)

Learn how Specsavers, one of the UK's most recognised high street brands, has turned to Fun Pro for [corporate entertainment](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/pages/office-and-corporate-entertainment) across multiple events over three years. From branded conference activations in Birmingham to evening celebrations in Southampton, this ongoing partnership demonstrates how Fun Pro delivers consistent, reliable entertainment for one of Britain's leading optical and hearing care providers.

## **Customer Profile**

Specsavers is a household name across the United Kingdom, operating within the Healthcare Services and Retail sectors. The company specialises in optical and hearing care, providing comprehensive services including eye tests, prescription glasses, contact lenses, hearing tests, and hearing aids through their extensive network of high street stores.

As a major employer with teams spread across the country, Specsavers regularly organises [corporate events](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/pages/promotional-event-games) to bring their people together, celebrate achievements, and foster team connections. Over the past three years, multiple departments within Specsavers have chosen Fun Pro to provide [interactive entertainment](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/category/interactive-game-hire) for their corporate gatherings, from large-scale conferences to intimate evening celebrations.

## **About Specsavers**

Specsavers has grown to become one of the most trusted names on the British high street since its founding in 1984. What began as a single optical practice has expanded into a global operation, though the brand remains synonymous with accessible, quality eye care and hearing services for millions of customers across the UK.

The company operates through a unique joint venture partnership model, with each store run by local optometrists and audiologists who have a genuine stake in their community. This approach has helped Specsavers maintain a personal touch whilst growing into a major retailer with thousands of employees nationwide.

Beyond their core retail operations, Specsavers invests significantly in their workforce. The company understands that engaged, motivated teams deliver better customer experiences. This commitment to their people extends to how they approach corporate events, whether large [conferences](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/pages/conference-idea) bringing together staff from across regions or smaller team celebrations marking milestones and achievements.

Like many large organisations, Specsavers faces the challenge of connecting dispersed teams. With stores and support functions spread throughout the UK, [corporate events](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/pages/promotional-event-games) serve as vital opportunities to build relationships, share knowledge, and celebrate collective successes. These gatherings require entertainment that appeals to diverse groups, breaks down barriers between colleagues who may rarely meet in person, and creates talking points that extend well beyond the event itself.

Finding a reliable entertainment partner who can deliver consistently across different venues, event types, and locations is no small task. The entertainment needs to be professional enough to reflect well on the Specsavers brand whilst being genuinely engaging and enjoyable for attendees of all ages and backgrounds.

## **Building a Partnership Through Consistent Delivery**

### **The Conference Challenge: ICC Birmingham, November 2022**

The partnership between Specsavers and Fun Pro began in November 2022 when the company was organising a conference at the ICC Birmingham, one of the UK's leading conference and events venues.

For this event, Specsavers required something beyond standard entertainment. They needed a [brand activation](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/pages/promotional-event-games) that would engage delegates, create energy during break periods, and reinforce their brand presence throughout the venue. The solution needed to be visually striking, competitively engaging, and capable of drawing people in throughout the day.

Fun Pro provided a branded [Grab a Grand machine](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/category/grab-a-grand) complete with a custom leaderboard. The Grab a Grand game, where participants step into an enclosed booth and attempt to grab as much prize money as possible whilst air swirls around them, creates instant excitement and draws crowds of spectators. By [branding](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/pages/branded-game-hire) the game with Specsavers' visual identity, the activation became an extension of the conference experience rather than simply an entertainment add-on.

The inclusion of a leaderboard added a competitive element that kept delegates engaged throughout the event. Participants could see how their efforts compared to colleagues, encouraging repeat attempts and generating conversation across different sessions. This combination of branded visual impact and competitive gameplay proved highly effective for the conference environment.

### **Team Entertainment: Bedfordshire, January 2023**

Just two months later, a different team within Specsavers approached Fun Pro for an evening event in Bedfordshire. This booking came through a separate member of staff, demonstrating how word of the Birmingham conference success had spread within the organisation.

The requirements for this event differed significantly from the conference activation. Rather than a single branded focal point, the team wanted a selection of games that would provide varied entertainment throughout an evening celebration. Fun Pro supplied an air hockey table, a dance machine, and a twin basketball game.

This combination offered something for different preferences and energy levels. The [air hockey table](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/category/exhibition-games/60/air-hockey-table-hire) provided head-to-head competition in a format familiar to most attendees. The [dance machine](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/category/arcade-games/39/dance-machine) brought high-energy entertainment that could involve spectators as much as participants. The twin [basketball game](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/category/arcade-games/6/electronic-basket-ball-hire) offered accessible competitive fun that colleagues could enjoy together.

The variety ensured that regardless of whether attendees preferred active competition or watching colleagues compete, there was entertainment to suit. This approach reflects Fun Pro's understanding that successful corporate entertainment must cater to diverse personalities within any team gathering.

### **Returning for More: Southampton, January 2025**

![](/images/specsavers-fun-pro-uk.jpg)

Two years later, Specsavers returned to Fun Pro for their most recent event, a corporate evening event held at the Hilton Hotel within Southampton's Utilita Bowl complex. This booking confirmed the ongoing trust that Specsavers places in Fun Pro's ability to deliver quality entertainment for their corporate gatherings.

For this event, Specsavers initially enquired about six different games: [shuffleboard](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/category/exhibition-games/87/shuffleboard-hire), [buzz wire](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/category/interactive-game-hire/9/giant-buzz-wire), [dance machine](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/category/arcade-games/39/dance-machine), [table football](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/category/corporate-entertainment/27/football-tables), Top Doc, and [air hockey](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/category/exhibition-games/60/air-hockey-table-hire). This expanded selection demonstrated the client's confidence in building a more comprehensive entertainment offering, knowing from previous experience that Fun Pro's equipment would arrive in excellent condition and operate reliably throughout the evening.

The game selection balanced different types of engagement. Shuffleboard and table football provided classic pub-style entertainment that encourages conversation whilst playing. The buzz wire game offered a test of steady hands and concentration that drew crowds of onlookers. The dance machine returned by popular demand, whilst air hockey provided the fast-paced competitive action that had proven successful at previous events. The games added variety to the mix, ensuring something fresh for those who may have attended previous Specsavers events featuring Fun Pro entertainment.
