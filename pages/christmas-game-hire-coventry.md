## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much space is required for Christmas game setup?

Space requirements differ by game selection. Our Coventry game hire team provides detailed specifications during booking and can conduct complimentary site assessments for local venues.

### Are Christmas games suitable for outdoor winter events?

Most Christmas games require indoor installation for equipment protection. However, we provide weatherproof alternatives for covered outdoor spaces. Contact our team to explore your specific venue requirements.

### How far in advance should I book Christmas games?

The festive season is understandably busy and books up fast, so we advise securing bookings 6-8 weeks ahead to ensure availability, particularly for December events in Coventry and surrounding areas.

### What power requirements do Christmas games need?

Most equipment operates on standard UK mains power (13amp socket). Our technical team will be able to confirm specific requirements and can arrange alternative power solutions when necessary.
