# Summer Entertainment Ideas

Liven up those long hot summer days with Fun Pro UK!  
  
If you're planning something special this summer then make sure you hire some of the games and other party equipment we have in stock. We have a huge range of entertainment available for hire that will really get everyone, from the youngest to the oldest, at your event in the party mood. What's more, because we deliver nationwide, wherever you are in the UK our team are able to deliver the good times to you.  
  
Summer is all about spending quality time outdoors with family and friends, and we have fun items that are absolutely perfect for gardens and other outside spaces. From brilliant children's bouncy castles that kids won't be able to resist, to [assault courses](/products/inflatable-assault-courses/#content) that always provide plenty of obstacles to test the skills of your guests, there are so many options to choose from with us.

## Inflatable Slides and Activities

Our [Super Slides](/products/super-slides/#content) are a perfect accompaniment to a day of summer fun. Once you clamber up to the top a quick descent awaits as you slip down that inflatable slope. Our slides feature double alleys, which means that two people can race down to the bottom at once. It's great entertainment and you'll find anyone who gives it a go won't be able to resist sampling the delight of flying back down to earth again and again.  
  
For something a tad more relaxed, but still a great test of mettle, be sure to check out our [Human Table Football](/products/human-table-football/#content) option. This is a great choice for an outdoor game and is something both young and old can get a real kick out of playing. Kids love an opportunity to play this supersized version of the classic [table football](/products/football-tables/#content) game - it's riotous fun that's almost as enjoyable to watch as it is to participate in.

## Giant Garden Games

If these products sound a bit too large for your back garden, then don't worry. We have a whole host of smaller party games that will fit in to the snuggest of spaces. These pack a lot of fun into their smaller footprint. Remember the game Jenga? You really need to check out our blown-up [Giant Garden Jenga](/products/giant-jenga-hire/#content) game. It's an excellent way to while away a warm afternoon. Or for some low-stakes challenges, why not [hire Giant Connect Four](/products/giant-connect-4-game-hire/#content)?  
  
Whatever you choose for your summer party, you'll be receiving a premium quality product that is really built to last. Everything we stock is commercial grade, and this means it is easily able to handle a summery afternoon of lively kids and grown-ups playing with it. But it's not just our amusements that are fantastic, our service is second-to-none too. No matter where you are in the UK, we will be at your address right on time to drop everything off for you. Our punctuality is just one of the reasons why we have such a fantastic reputation in the entertainment hire industry.
