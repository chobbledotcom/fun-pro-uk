# Fundraising Event ideas

Looking for a way to really liven up a fundraising event? The party equipment hire experts at Fun Pro UK have plenty of options for you.  
  
When you're raising money for a good cause, games are a great way to get people dipping into their pockets. Giving people entertainment that keeps them coming back for more means that at the end of the day your coffers will be bulging with plenty of cash for whatever charity you're collecting for.

## Entertainment and Attractions for Fundraisers

Games which tickle that competitive spirit are an excellent choice, and we have a great variety for you to choose from. [Air hockey](../category/arcade-games/60/air-hockey-table-hire.html "Air Hockey Table Hire") and [table football](../category/interactive-game-hire/27/football-tables.html "FOOTBALL TABLES") will have your attendees facing off against each other to see who possesses the most skills. People just can't resist the opportunity to step up and have a go at these arcade classics. Why not organise a full-scale tournament with a prize at the end for the victor? This is sure to be a great way to keep the crowd engaged while raising plenty of money for you worthy cause.  
  
The possibility of a prize is an excellent way to tempt people to part with a bit of cash. At Fun Pro UK we've got an extensive range of games that will have people lining up to try their luck at. The [Prize Crane Grabber](../prize-crane-arcade-grabber.html "Prize Crane Arcade Grabber") is a classic machine that people just love. Participants will really enjoy attempting to get that metal claw to pick up the prize they desire most, and if they want it enough you'll find they'll be willing to pay multiple times to try to get it!  
  
Another great option for fundraisers is the [Crack the Code safe cracker game](../crack-the-code-safe-cracker.html "Crack The Code safe cracker"). Your guests simply pay a fee for an opportunity to guess the code to open the Perspex box and claim the prize inside. Whatever that prize is, is entirely up to you! Pick something tempting enough and you'll find people will pay to guess again and again and again, which will be fantastic news for whatever you're fundraising for.
