# Conference Entertainment Ideas

Want to add some excitement to a conference or similar [corporate event](/category/corporate-entertainment/)? Let Fun Pro UK take care of it!

Entertainment is a great way to enable guests and delegates to have a bit of fun during breaks and at the end of the day at conferences in the UK. What's more, it provides an excellent opportunity for people to break the ice and socialise.

We deliver nationwide, so no matter where your conference is located in the UK, we can bring our high-quality games and other products to you. We've provided numerous corporate clients with our amusements at venues such as the [NEC in Birmingham](nec-birmingham/), the [ExCeL in London](excel-london/) and the [Manchester Central Convention Complex](manchester-central-convention-complex/), so we know the exact level of professionalism people hosting conferences expect from our service.

So, what kind of fun can we deliver to you? Here's a few examples:

## Racing Car Simulator

High-octane thrills are available in abundance from our superb [Racing Car Simulator](/category/interactive-game-hire/twin-racing-car-simulators/). Any petrolheads in attendance at your conference won't be able to resist the temptation to take a seat and test their skills on one of the numerous tracks we can provide with our cutting-edge computer.

All the action is visible on a superb quality screen, and this will be sure to draw a crowd of spectators and budding Formula One drivers wanting to show off their talent to their fellow delegates. This [arcade game](/category/arcade-games/lights-out-game/) attraction is always such a hit at conferences and similar occasions that you'll be sure to be hiring it again and again for all your corporate events.

## Rodeo Bull

Transport everyone at your conference to the plains of the Wild West with this fantastic [Rodeo Bull](/category/fun-days/46/rodeo-bull/). Once your cowboys or cowgirls climb aboard the bull they'll have to use all their skills of balance and weight distribution to remain seated upon it as it jerks and rotates in different directions and at rapidly changing speeds. It's a very tricky challenge, but fortunately, once they (invevitably) fall off a soft landing awaits on the padded inflatable floor.

The Rodeo Bull is a real classic that never fails to get people loosening up and in a good mood. Add this [interactive game](/interactive-game-hire/) to your next conference and watch how many people want to have a go!

## Buzz Wire

Plenty of nerve and a very steady hand is required to conquer this [prize game](/category/prize-games/). Your guests will love the opportunity to test how calm they can remain under pressure with our brilliant [Giant Buzz Wire](/category/interactive-game-hire/giant-buzz-wire/). Everyone knows how this amusement works, simply get from one end of the wire to the other with the provided loop without setting the buzzer off. It's so simple to explain, yet so fiendishly difficult to complete!

This versatile game is great because it takes up so little space, despite being so massive that there's no way anyone will miss it. It's a great diversion for your delegates in between sessions, so be sure you don't forget to add it to your next conference.
