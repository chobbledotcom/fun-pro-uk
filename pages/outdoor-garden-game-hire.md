## Inflatable Slides and Activities

Our [Super Slides](/category/fun-days/26/super-slides.html "SUPER SLIDES") are a perfect accompaniment to a day of summer fun. Once you clamber up to the top a quick descent awaits as you slip down that inflatable slope. Our slides feature double alleys, which means that two people can race down to the bottom at once. It's great entertainment and you'll find anyone who gives it a go won't be able to resist sampling the delight of flying back down to earth again and again.  
  
For something a tad more relaxed, but still a great test of mettle, be sure to check out our [Human Table Football](/category/fun-days/human-table-football.html "HUMAN TABLE FOOTBALL") option. This is a great choice for an outdoor game and is something both young and old can get a real kick out of playing. Kids love an opportunity to play this supersized version of the classic [table football](/category/interactive-game-hire/27/football-tables.html "FOOTBALL TABLES") game - it's riotous fun that's almost as enjoyable to watch as it is to participate in.
