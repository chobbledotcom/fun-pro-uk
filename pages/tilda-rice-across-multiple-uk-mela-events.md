## About Tilda Rice

Tilda Rice has been a trusted name in British households for decades, bringing premium rice and grain products to families across the UK. The company's journey began with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality rice that enhances family meals and brings people together around the dinner table. Over the years, Tilda has grown from a specialist importer to a comprehensive food brand that understands the cultural significance of rice in diverse communities.

The challenge facing Tilda was how to meaningfully connect with the vibrant multicultural communities that form a significant part of their customer base. Traditional advertising could reach these audiences, but the company recognised that authentic engagement required something more personal and experiential. Mela events, which celebrate South Asian culture through food, music, dance, and community activities, represented the perfect opportunity to connect with families in a natural, celebratory environment.

However, standing out at these bustling community festivals presented its own challenges. With numerous food vendors, cultural displays, and entertainment options competing for attention, Tilda needed something that would draw people to their stand and create lasting positive associations with their brand. The company had previous experience working with FunPro UK several years earlier and remembered the professional service and creative solutions they provided.

When planning their return to community event marketing, Tilda knew they needed an [activation event](/promotion-event-games/) that would reflect their brand values whilst creating genuine excitement. The rice and grain sector isn't traditionally associated with high-energy entertainment, but Tilda understood that connecting with families meant creating moments of joy and shared experience that would resonate long after the festival ended.

## The Community Engagement Challenge

As told to FunPro by the Tilda Rice marketing team.

The Mela circuit represents some of the most vibrant and well-attended cultural celebrations in the UK, but these events also present unique marketing challenges. "We needed something that would capture the family-friendly spirit of these festivals whilst clearly connecting back to our brand," explained the Tilda team. "These aren't corporate environments where traditional marketing approaches work. Families come to Mela events to celebrate their culture, enjoy authentic food, and have fun together."

The outdoor nature of these events created additional complexity. Most Mela celebrations take place in parks or open spaces, with vendors operating under marquees that need to accommodate unpredictable British weather. "The ground conditions are rarely ideal," noted the events team. "We needed entertainment that could work effectively on uneven surfaces whilst maintaining safety standards for families with children of all ages."

Tilda's previous experience with FunPro UK several years earlier had left a positive impression, particularly regarding their ability to handle logistical challenges whilst maintaining high standards of presentation. When the company decided to increase their community event presence, they contacted FunPro UK directly to discuss options for creating [branded entertainment](/category/branded-exhibition-games/) that would work across multiple locations.

The initial enquiry focused on the [Roll and Bowl](/category/all-products/51/roll-and-bowl-game-hire/) game, which had proven popular in their previous collaboration. However, this time Tilda wanted to take the branding much further. "We didn't just want our logo on the game," the marketing team explained. "We wanted the entire experience to feel authentically Tilda whilst being genuinely entertaining for festival-goers."

## The Bespoke Branding Solution

As told to FunPro by the Tilda Rice brand activation team.

FunPro UK's in-house print team worked closely with Tilda to create a completely customised version of their popular Roll and Bowl game. Every visual element was redesigned to incorporate Tilda's colourful rice bag designs, creating an immediately recognisable brand experience that felt natural within the game's mechanics.

"The transformation was remarkable," recalled the Tilda team. "The well-known colourful Tilda Rice bags became the characters that move across the game as contestants play. It wasn't just branded entertainment; it was entertainment that told our brand story through play." The customisation extended to every component: the rear board featured Tilda's signature colours and messaging, the playing fields incorporated rice-themed graphics, and the front skirt created a cohesive brand environment that drew people in from across the festival grounds.

The technical execution required careful consideration of outdoor conditions. FunPro UK's team addressed the uneven ground challenges by providing professional levelling solutions and ensuring the game remained stable and safe throughout each event. "They understood that our reputation was on the line," noted the marketing team. "If the game didn't work properly or looked unprofessional, it would reflect poorly on our brand."

The logistics of moving this customised setup across four different cities also required careful planning. Each location presented its own access challenges, from narrow park gates to restricted vehicle access times. FunPro UK's experience with [nationwide event coverage](/delivery-areas/) proved invaluable, with their team coordinating delivery and setup to ensure each event opened with the game fully operational and looking perfect.
