# Party Games Hire

**Cheer Up Your Friends, Family, or Colleagues with Party Games Hire**

Do we really need an excuse to have a party?

In the past, we’d celebrate a birthday, wedding, or promotion with a big or small party. Corporations may celebrate the addition of a new client, merger, or higher profits with a party for their employees.

But during today’s times, we can just celebrate!

And what better way than to just have a party for no reason, just to get together and have fun with everyone? But you’re still going to need entertainment at your next event.

Party games hire is a great way to keep people happy and entertained while they celebrate together.

**What Types of Entertainment Should I Get?**

A DJ can be hired to play party music, or you can get indoor party games hire so that your party goers will have some activities. If you’re inviting people to the party who may not know each all that well, often people may feel out of place.

When you get large party games for hire, you’ll be providing entertainment and fun for your guests. Playing games together will work as an ice-breaker, so people can have fun and make new friends.

Activities are also a healthier way to have a party than just offering food and drink for several hours.

**What Types of Indoor Party Games Hire Can I Book?**

Arcade games are perfect for indoors. Guests can play alone or compete against each other.

Points can be tallied to determine who is the big winner by the end of the night. Other group activities can involve twin racing car simulators or the giant buzz wire.

**What Types of Outdoor Party Games for Hire Can I Book?**

If your event is going to be outdoors, there are some games that are also suitable for that that don’t need electrical power, or worries about the rain.

The human table football can be played in groups. There’s also the Dash & Grab or Hungry Hippo, which is an inflatable game that can be played outdoors.

**Are There Non-Competitive Types of Fun?**

If you want to provide party games for hire for everyone, you may also wish to have activities that are non-competitive. Some of these activities are just for fun and to provide a fun element to your party or event.

The Dance Machine is a great way to have some fun dancing, while following the moves on the screen.

There is no competition on this one. Green Screen Photo Booths are also another great way to have party fun. Your attendees will be able to take home photos of themselves.

**Are There Games for Adults, Teens, and Children?**

Yes, there are games for everyone, whether child, teen, or adult. Remember that adults will have fun playing children’s games too.

A popular indoor party games hire is the Top Doc Operation game, very similar to that childhood Operation board game.

Adults will still enjoy playing arcade games too, or more modern versions of sporting events, such as human table football or football tables. Some games may be more suitable for younger children. We can assist you with the age ranges if you’re puzzled.

**When Should I Have the Party Games During an Event?**

It’s up to you. You may wish to designate a specific time for the party games. For example, perhaps you want everyone to gather together for a speech to thank them for their service.

You can use yellow ribbon barriers to block off the games until people can officially play. If you want to have food and drink, it may be best to allow people to eat first, then play the games, or to play the games, shut them down, and then eat.

You don’t want to mix food and drink in with the games.

You can make up a schedule or itinerary in advance, so people know what to expect when they arrive. If you do decide to have the games for the length of the event, then we suggest that you set up the food and drink away from the games, so that people don’t bring it near the party games and risk spilling it over the equipment.

**What Kinds of Locations Can I Have for Party Games?**

You can have them in your backyard, or at a corporation’s building, inside a conference room. If you’re renting a banquet hall, the games can be set up there, at a convention centre, or in an outdoor field.

The main concern with outdoor party games hire, or indoor party games hire, is that you have enough space for the game, but also enough space for the games’ players, and onlookers.

There may also be health protocols in place, now or in the future where people may need to be spaced apart.
