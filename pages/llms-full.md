### Product Categories

#### Arcade Games
Fun Pro UK offers retro and modern arcade game hire for events across the UK. Options include classic multi-game arcade cabinets, racing simulators, sports games, and themed machines. Arcade games are popular at corporate events, parties, and university events, providing a nostalgic and engaging entertainment option for guests of all ages.

#### Prize Games
Fun Pro UK supplies prize game hire including prize wheels, skill-based games, and chance-based machines where guests can win small prizes or tokens. These are popular at corporate events for staff incentives, exhibitions for lead generation rewards, and fundraising events.

#### Pub Games
Fun Pro UK offers traditional pub game hire including shuffleboard, skittles, giant Jenga, and other classic formats. Pub games work well for office socials, evening entertainment, team building events, and summer parties where guests want relaxed, social interaction.

#### Grab A Grand
Fun Pro UK supplies cash grabber machine hire — the classic money booth where participants step inside and try to grab as many notes as possible within a time limit. Popular at corporate events, promotional campaigns, and exhibitions as a high-impact, crowd-drawing centrepiece.

#### Batak
Fun Pro UK offers Batak reaction wall hire — the professional-grade electronic reaction testing machine that challenges participants to hit illuminated targets as quickly as possible. The Batak Pro is widely used at exhibitions, corporate events, and university events to attract attention, generate competition, and create a measurable challenge.

#### Roll & Bowl
Fun Pro UK supplies roll and bowl game hire including the classic donkey derby format, where participants roll balls down a lane to move their character along a race track. This format works well as a competitive group activity at family fun days, corporate events, and fundraisers.

#### Fun Fair Stalls
Fun Pro UK offers traditional funfair stall hire including hook-a-duck, tin can alley, splat the rat, and other fairground-style games. Funfair stalls are a versatile option for outdoor events, summer fairs, school fetes, fundraisers, and corporate fun days.

#### Fun Foods
Fun Pro UK provides fun food machine hire including ice cream vans, candy floss machines, popcorn machines, hot dog carts, and slush machines. Fun food options add a catering and entertainment element to events and are popular at outdoor summer events, school fairs, and corporate fun days.

#### Christmas Grotto Hire
Fun Pro UK supplies complete Santa's grotto setups for hire, including decorations, props, and optional staffing. Christmas grottos are popular for office Christmas parties, school Christmas fairs, shopping centre activations, and community events during the festive season.

#### Photo Booths & Magic Mirrors
Fun Pro UK offers photo booth and magic mirror hire for events. Both options provide an interactive, shareable experience for guests and include printed photo strips or digital sharing. Magic mirrors feature touchscreen animation and personalised messages. Both are popular at weddings, corporate parties, and award evenings.

Fun Pro UK provides inflatable assault courses, giant inflatables, and activity games suitable for university freshers fairs and large outdoor events. These high-impact options attract student attention and generate queues and footfall at freshers events.
