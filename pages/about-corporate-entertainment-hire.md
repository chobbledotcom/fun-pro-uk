# **The team to trust for nationwide**corporate fun hire**

Welcome to the Fun Pro UK website, we are a family business and have worked in the entertainment industry providing Interactive Game Hire since 2009 and take pride in delivering amazing memorable fun in a professional way to our clients across the UK. Based in Coventry the heart of the Midlands within easy access to motorway links we are well situated to cover the whole of the UK.

## **The Team to Trust**

We only use the very highest quality equipment, and hand pick our Fun Pro team members to ensure a consistently high standard of service we can help your company to stand out from the competition.

We are always looking at our core values and how we can add to our games portfolio whilst continuing to provide quality games, great value, reliability and top customer service.

## **High-quality Equipment**

We believe that your event should be enjoyable, but your safety is of paramount importance to us and we use the very highest quality equipment, which is regularly tested and thoroughly cleaned after every hire, you can be assured that whatever you hire will reflect well on your company. Our team also carry a full range of spares and additional equipment in the unlikely event that anything does go wrong, we can quickly fix it and your event can continue. Naturally, we hold full public liability insurance and will be happy to work with you on any risk assessments needed for your event.

## **Unique industry understanding**

We have been working in the entertainment industry for many years, and we know what works and how to make sure that everyone has fun. Our friendly team are happy to share that experience with you, to help you plan the best possible event for your venue, your audience and your budget. Look around our website for some great [corporate entertainment](/category/corporate-entertainment/) ideas or if you're not sure where to start, then talk to our friendly Fun Pro team today to start planning your next event.
