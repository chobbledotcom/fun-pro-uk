## Christmas Game Hire: Common FAQs

### How much space is required for Christmas game setup?

Space requirements vary by game. Our Birmingham game hire specialists provide comprehensive specifications during booking and can perform free site evaluations for local venues if needed.

### Are Christmas games suitable for outdoor winter events?

Most Christmas games require indoor setup for equipment safety. However, we offer weather-resistant options for sheltered outdoor areas. [Contact our team](contact-fun-pro-uk.html) to discuss your specific needs.

### How far in advance should I book Christmas games?

The Christmas period is naturally high-demand and fills quickly, so we recommend confirming bookings 6-8 weeks in advance to guarantee availability, especially for December events in Birmingham and nearby locations.

### What power requirements do Christmas games need?

Most equipment works on standard UK mains power (13amp socket). Our technical specialists will confirm specific requirements.
