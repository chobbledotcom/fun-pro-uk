## Frequently Asked Questions About Christmas Game Hire

### What space is needed for Christmas game installation?

Space needs vary according to game choices. Our London game hire specialists provide comprehensive specifications during reservation and offer complimentary venue assessments for local locations.

### Can Christmas games work for outdoor winter celebrations?

Most Christmas games require an indoor setup for equipment safety. Nevertheless, we offer weather-resistant options for sheltered outdoor areas. [Contact our team](contact-fun-pro-uk/) to discuss your particular venue needs.

### When should I reserve Christmas games?

The festive season is naturally in high demand and fills quickly, so we recommend confirming bookings 6-8 weeks early to guarantee availability, especially for December events in London and neighbouring regions.

### What electrical requirements do Christmas games have?

Most equipment requires standard UK mains power (13amp socket). Our technical specialists will confirm the exact requirements.
