    image: /images/products/prize-wheel/prize-wheel-3.jpg
    body: >-
      The Spin the Wheel game involves a circular wheel on a stand. The player
      stands near it, gently grabs the side and makes it spin. The wheel spins
      until it stops, and the arrow at the top of the stand points to a prize
      number. This number can correspond to a prize list that you create.


      What makes it extra fun is that the wheel is divided up into coloured
      sections. You can create a poster listing all the prizes for the numbers.
      The even numbers can result in a no-win, while the odd numbers can result
      in a win. Smaller numbers can involve a small prize, while the big number
      could win the player something special. Another option is that the numbers
      correspond to numbered bags containing a small prize.


      Prize ideas include branded company merchandise such as a pen, pencil,
      notebook, or key ring. One of the prizes could be a gift certificate to a
      local restaurant or shop. The wheel can run continuously throughout your
      event or be used for scheduled prize-giving moments.
