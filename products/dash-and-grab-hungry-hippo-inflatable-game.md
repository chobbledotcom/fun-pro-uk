**What Is the Hungry Hippo Game?**

This is a game that takes safety and fun into consideration. The game is set up on a huge inflatable stage. The premise is that there’s a hungry hippo.

You must feed him plenty of food (balls) to win the game. But you’re up against a wide range of other competitors too. This game allows four people to play at once.

But there’s more to it than simply hopping around the bouncy stage to grab the balls.

You’re actually tethered to the back rails so you have a limited space to play. While this keeps the game safe so you don’t bounce into others, the inflatable hungry hippos hold you back from achieving your goals.
