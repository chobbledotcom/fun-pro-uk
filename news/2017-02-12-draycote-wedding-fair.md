# 12 February 17 - Draycote wedding fair

Team Perfect Snap are getting excited for the Wedding Inspo Evening which we are attending this a Thursday 16th February 2027 at the [Draycote Hotel in Rugby, Warwickshire.](/delivery-areas/#content)

We will be there with our Green Screen Dream Machine and marvellous talking [Magic Selfie Mirror](/products/magic-mirror/#content) for you all to see!  
The evening will begin at 19.30, finishing at 21.30. On arrival there will be a complementary glass of bubbly, lots of demonstrations to take part in and items to sample.  
During the event there will be a range of companies showcasing a variety of services for your wedding day. To name but a few there will be Make My Day wedding planning services, Nanna childcare agency along with Salters of Rugby.  
Perfect Snap will invite you to try our [Photo Booth and Magic Mirror](/categories/photo-booths-and-magic-mirrors/#content) as we firmly believe you need to try before you buy! What better opportunity is there for the bride to be to have a selfie taken with either the mother of the bride and the bridesmaids!  
We look forward to seeing you there!
