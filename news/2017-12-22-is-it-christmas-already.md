Is It [Christmas](/categories/christmas-game-hire/#content) already?

We can't believe that Christmas really is around the corner now. We've been so busy helping to celebrate at wonderful parties, it's really crept up on us!

We've had a very busy 2017 and have recently taken delivery of some wonderful new products for you to hire, ensuring any event you host will be fun and entertaining.

This year we've seen our [Selfie Mirrors](/products/magic-mirror/#content) and [Green Screen Photo Booths](/products/green-screen-photo-booths/#content) become even more popular. Hiring these at private parties and corporate functions have bought great fun and laughter.

Everyone loves a selfie, right?

Perfect Snap have also taken delivery of a few new products this year. Our Social Media Pod has been a real hit for those wanting to engage and attract new clients on exhibition stands, conferences and meetings.

This stand-alone pod can be branded with your company logo and lets your clients, customers or guests takes their selfie and then instantly share it to social media! They can also email then to their personal email.

With this great new product and sharing capability, your guests and clients will always remember your event.

Our interactive games have been a huge hit too. The 'Beat the Bleep' competitive speed game where you play head to head with your opponent hitting the flashing buttons to beat the clock, was very popular at an event last week.

As was our huge buzz wire game and not forgetting our 1980's retro arcade game which has definitely brought back childhood memories of Pacman and Space Invaders.

We'd like to take this little opportunity to thank you for being a customer of Perfect Snap this year.

We can't wait to see you all again in 2018.

Merry Christmas and a happy New Year!
