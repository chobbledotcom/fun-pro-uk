# 31 July 17 - Weekend of 29th July 2017

Wow! I can't tell you what a busy weekend we have had here at Perfect Snap. We seem to have been all over the place! On Friday we were at a beautiful wedding with a [green screen photo booth](/products/green-screen-photo-booths/) and our spectacular LOVE letters at Ardencote Manor, Warwickshire. This was a gorgeous venue where the guests were really in the mood to party! Our LOVE letters made a fabulous back drop shining brightly in the background. A 'lovely' effect!  
  
Saturday saw us at a few venues in and around Warwickshire and [Birmingham](/locations/birmingham/corporate-event-hire/) not forgetting Evesham too!. The first of our photo booths went to a wedding reception at Jephson Gardens, Leamington Spa, Warwickshire. Our green screen dream machine photo booth and the vintage retro themed outer skin was chosen by the bride for the entertainment during the evening reception. This looked amazing at the venue which over looks the park.  
  
Our popular team member Ross was out with a Magic Selfie Mirror at the extravagant venue of Aston Villa Football Club, Birmingham. The guests had a great time at the wedding reception using our Mirror to have a selfie or two! It was a wonderful outlook onto the pitch from the room the special couple had [hired for wedding entertainment](/categories/weddings/). Ross even managed to have a cheeky selfie himself with the football pitch behind him.  
  
Warwickshire Golf Club, Warwickshire held yet another wedding reception last night where one of our Magic Selfie Mirrors took centre stage for the evening entertainment. The guests were very busy using our great prop box for their photographs and then sticking their selfies into the guest book we provided - it looked amazing, lots of pictures for the bride and groom to look back on of their special day.  
  
Another green screen photo booth with the silver chesterfield outer skin arrived at Badsey Cricket Club near [Evesham](/delivery-areas/) for an evening wedding reception. Again this went down a storm! Everyone adores our photo booths, they are fun and provide great entertainment and also gives the guests a memory of an instant print photo to take away with them. The props were out all ready for the guests to use as they waited to enter the booth.  
  
Perfect Snap were busy that is for sure! - That is how we like it,!  
We would like to take this opportunity to say a massive 'Congratulations' to all of the happy couples we have had the honour in meeting this weekend and thank you for using Perfect Snap.
