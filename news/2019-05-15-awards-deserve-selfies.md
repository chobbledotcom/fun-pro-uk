**AWARDS & SELFIES!**

The Managers attended their company conference during the day at a hotel venue on the outskirts of Rugby, Warwickshire. The Green Screen Dream Machine Photobooth was set up and ready to get the party started for 7.30pm. This gave everyone a chance to put their party frocks on and practice their best cheesy grin! Our prop box was over flowing with colourful glasses, hand held signs and silly hats.
