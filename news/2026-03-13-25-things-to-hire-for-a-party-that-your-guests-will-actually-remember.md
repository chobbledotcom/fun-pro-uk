Planning a party can feel like a juggling act. You have the venue sorted, the food organised, and the guest list finalised, but something is still missing. The entertainment. Getting this part right is often the difference between an event people politely attend and one they genuinely talk about afterwards.

Whether you are organising a [corporate celebration](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/events/corporate-events/), a milestone birthday, a [wedding](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/events/wedding-entertainment/) reception, or a [team building](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/events/team-building-entertainment/) event, hiring the right entertainment takes the guesswork out of keeping your guests engaged. Professional party entertainment hire means everything arrives on time, gets set up for you, and is collected afterwards, so you can focus on enjoying the event rather than managing logistics.

This guide covers 25 of the best things to hire for a party, broken down by category so you can quickly find what suits your event, your audience, and your venue. Each option includes practical advice on why it works and which types of events it suits best.

## **Interactive Games That Get Everyone Involved**

<p style="text-align: center"><img src="/images/interactive-games-fun-pro-uk.jpg"></p>

[Interactive games](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/interactive-game-hire/) are one of the most effective ways to bring guests together at any event. They create natural conversation starters, encourage friendly competition, and give people something to do beyond standing around with a drink. The best interactive games suit a range of ages and abilities, so nobody feels left out.

### **1\. Racing Car Simulators**

Few things draw a crowd quite like a pair of [racing simulators](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/vr-race-car-simulator/#content). Guests sit down, grab the steering wheel, and compete head-to-head on a real-time leaderboard. Because they are seated, racing simulators are accessible to guests of all abilities and comfortable for people who might not want to be physically active. They work brilliantly at corporate parties, milestone birthdays, and wedding evening receptions where you want a competitive edge without anyone breaking a sweat.

### **2\. Batak Reaction Games**

[Batak Pro](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/batak-pro/#content) and [Batak Lite](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/batak-lite/#content) are reaction-speed games where players hit illuminated targets as quickly as possible within a set time. It naturally attracts a crowd of spectators, creates friendly rivalry through the leaderboard, and is one of the most popular choices for corporate events and team building parties. A Batak game can be [fully branded](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/branded-game-hire/) with your company logo, making it a practical choice for [brand activations](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/events/brand-activation/) and exhibitions too.

### **3\. Giant Buzz Wire**

The [Giant Buzz Wire](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/giant-buzz-wire/) takes the classic steady-hand game and scales it up to a full-sized challenge. It is low-pressure, nostalgic, and works as a brilliant conversation starter. Guests tend to gather round to watch others attempt the course, which creates exactly the kind of natural interaction you want at a party. It suits family celebrations, [fun days](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/events/family-fun-days/), and office parties equally well.

### **4\. Electronic Basketball**

[Electronic basketball](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/electronic-basket-ball-hire/) is fast-paced, intuitive, and appeals to competitive guests of all ages. Rounds are quick, so there is no long waiting time, and the scoring system adds a layer of friendly rivalry. It works particularly well at corporate parties, [university freshers events](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/events/university-events/), and family celebrations where you need something that draws people in without requiring much explanation.

### **5\. Whack-a-Mole**

There is something deeply satisfying about [Whack-a-Mole](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/whack-a-mole-game-hire/) that appeals to everyone from children to senior executives. It is instantly recognisable, requires no instruction, and can be [fully customised](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/branded-game-hire/) with your own branding or theme. This makes it a strong choice for brand activations and corporate fun days as well as private celebrations.

## **Photo Booths and Magic Mirrors**

<p style="text-align: center"><img src="/images/photo-booths.png"></p>

Photo entertainment consistently ranks among the most popular things to hire for a party, and for good reason. Guests leave with a physical keepsake, group photos encourage people to mingle, and the results often end up on social media, extending the reach of your event long after the last guest has left.

### **6\. Photo Booths**

A classic [photo booth](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/classic-photo-booths/#content) with props, backdrops, and instant prints remains one of the most reliable party hire choices. Modern photo booths can include green screen technology, allowing guests to choose from a range of digital backgrounds. They work at virtually any event, from corporate celebrations and awards evenings to weddings and birthday parties. It is worth considering where you position the booth, as placing it near the bar or main social area tends to encourage more use throughout the evening.

### **7\. Magic Mirrors (Selfie Mirrors)**

[Magic Mirrors](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/magic-mirror/) offer a more interactive alternative to traditional photo booths. Guests stand in front of a full-length mirror that guides them through animations, filters, and customisable frames before printing their photo. The touchscreen element adds a layer of engagement, and the larger format means bigger groups can get in the shot together. They are particularly popular at weddings, milestone birthdays, and corporate events where you want something that feels a little more premium.

### **8\. Green Screen Experiences**

Green screen setups let guests transport themselves to any location or themed background you choose. This is especially effective at themed events, brand activations, or Christmas parties where you want to create a cohesive visual experience. Guests can take home prints featuring branded or themed backdrops, creating a tangible reminder of the event.

## **Prize Games and Fun Fair Stalls**

![](/images/fun-fair-stalls.jpg)

There is something about [fairground-style entertainment](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/fun-fair-stalls/) that creates instant energy at an event. Guests step inside and have a set amount of time to grab as much as they can. It creates an instant crowd of spectators, generates excitement, and works brilliantly as a prize giveaway mechanic at corporate events, awards evenings, and brand activations. ### **10\. Prize Crane (Claw Machine)**

Fill it with branded merchandise, party favours, or small prizes and it becomes a self-contained entertainment hub that guests return to throughout the event. It is a particularly clever choice for product launches, wedding receptions, and corporate celebrations where you want to distribute gifts in an engaging way.

### **11\. Hook-a-Duck and Fun Fair Stalls**

Traditional fun fair stalls like [Hook-a-Duck](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/hook-a-duck/), Coconut Shy, and Tin Can Alley bring a relaxed, accessible energy to any party. They are low-pressure, suitable for younger guests and families, and create a village fete atmosphere that works well at summer parties, fun days, school events, and outdoor wedding receptions.

### **12\. Prize Wheel (Spin to Win)**

A [Prize Wheel](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/prize-wheel/) builds anticipation with every spin. You can customise the segments with your own prizes, brand messaging, or event-specific rewards, making it a versatile choice for exhibitions, corporate parties, and promotional events. ## **How to Choose the Right Entertainment for Your Party**

With so many options available, choosing the right mix of entertainment can feel like a challenge in itself. Here are some practical considerations that will help you narrow down the choices and create an event that works for your specific audience and venue.

**Consider your audience.** Think about the age range, physical abilities, and personalities of your guests. A [corporate party](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/events/corporate-events/) with senior executives will have different entertainment needs to a [family fun day](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/events/family-fun-days/) with children. The best approach is usually to offer a mix of high-energy and relaxed options so there is something for everyone.

**Think about your venue.** Indoor and outdoor venues each have their own considerations. Check dimensions carefully, particularly ceiling height for any games that need vertical space. Think about power access, floor surfaces, and whether your venue has any restrictions on noise or equipment. If you are planning an outdoor event, it is worth discussing wet weather contingencies with your supplier.

**Match entertainment to your event's purpose.** A networking event benefits from games that encourage conversation and interaction, like [pub games](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/pub-games/) or [photo booths](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/products/classic-photo-booths/#content). A celebration might call for higher-energy options like racing simulators or dance machines. A team building event works well with competitive games that feature leaderboards. Understanding what you want the entertainment to achieve will help you make better choices.

**Mix it up.** The most successful events combine different types of entertainment. Consider pairing a couple of high-energy [interactive games](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/categories/interactive-game-hire/) with a photo booth, some relaxed pub games, and a fun food option. This creates natural flow and ensures guests with different preferences all find something they enjoy.

**Plan for logistics.** Ask your supplier about [delivery](https://www.funprouk.co.uk/locations/) and setup times, space requirements, and what is included in the hire. Professional entertainment companies will handle delivery, installation, and collection as part of the service, which is one less thing for you to manage on the day. It is also worth booking early, particularly for popular dates like the Christmas party season or summer months.
