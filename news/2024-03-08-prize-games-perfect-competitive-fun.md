# [08 March 24 - Prize Games - Perfect Competitive Fun!](/prize-games-perfect-competitive-fun/)

Planning an event this year? Whether it's a [corporate event](/category/corporate-entertainment/), a trade show, or a private party, creating an atmosphere of fun and competition can significantly enhance the guest experience. This is where our range [prize games](/category/prize-games/) come into their own, offering entertainment alongside the thrill of competition and the joy of winning.

Prize games are the heart and soul of interactive entertainment, bringing people together through the universal language of play.

Some of our most popular prize games for hire include:

*   [Cash Grabber - Grab a Grand](/category/interactive-game-hire/3/grab-a-grand/): Step into the whirlwind where skill and luck meet. Perfect for [exhibition stands](/category/exhibition-games/) and similar events, it's a visually spectacular way to engage participants, offering them the chance to 'grab' branded vouchers or prizes
*   [Beat The Buzzer](/category/interactive-game-hire/62/beat-the-buzzer/): A test of steadiness and nerve, Beat The Buzzer and the [Giant Buzz Wire](/category/interactive-game-hire/giant-buzz-wire/) challenge players to move a wand along a charged wire without touching it. If anyone can make it to the end, a prize is well-deserved!
*   [High Striker](/category/arcade-games/48/high-striker/): Bring the funfair to your event with the classic High Striker. A hit at outdoor events and fun days, it allows everyone to test their strength in the most traditional of ways – with the reward of ringing that bell at the top.
*   [Plinko Game Hire](/category/all-products/68/plinko-game-hire/): Inspired by the iconic game show, Plinko is a game of chance that's always a crowd-pleaser. Participants drop chips down a pegged board, eagerly anticipating where they will land. It's perfect for injecting a bit of luck into your event, with various prizes up for grabs.
*   [Prize Wheel](/prize-wheel/): Spin the wheel and let fate decide! The Prize Wheel is a versatile game that can be custom-branded and used to distribute a wide range of prizes. It's an engaging way to draw attendees to your stand or add excitement to your party.

What sets these games apart is the opportunity for customisation. With [branded exhibition games](/category/branded-exhibition-games/), you can transform any of the above into a powerful promotional tool. Incorporating your company's branding into the game design will boost its visual appeal and improve brand recognition and recall, making your event a strategic marketing opportunity.
