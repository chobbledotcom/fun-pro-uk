# 12 September 18 - The importance of throwing the company Christmas party

We know it is only September and you don't really want to be thinking about the company Christmas party yet. But you need to get to it early to secure yourselves the best corporate entertainment available.

Here at Fun Pro UK we understand the importance of throwing a [company Christmas party](/categories/corporate-entertainment/). Check out our top reasons for why you should host a staff party this Christmas.

*   Shows the company's appreciation for their staff's hard work over the last year, which often leads to teams feeling more motivated and therefore increasing achievement for the following year.
*   Helps to boosts morale and strengthens communication and teamwork within departments.
*   Helps staff retention as they have good memories of the company to look back on.

However, we recognise that not all companies have huge pots of money to host a [Christmas event](/categories/christmas-game-hire/). So why not think about having a party during a lunch time within the office. This way more people may be able to attend rather than an evening event, and you would still see the benefits of rewarding your staff.

Alternatively, you could push back the party until January when more staff may be free as everyone knows December is a very busy month. This could also give your staff something to look forward to in the new year, to wipe away those post-Christmas blues.
