# 16 March 20 - FUN PRO UK AND COVID-19

Fun Pro Uk and COVID-19

We are continuing to work hard with our clients and venues to support everyone in this uncertain time that we are all experiencing.

Our main aim here at Fun Pro UK is always the health and safety of our clients.

We are currently receiving up to date advice from the UK Government and will be guided by the advice issued.

We understand that this is a difficult time in our industry but are working to re-schedule your events for the future.

We are here to support all of our clients just as we have been before and will continue to do so from our homes.
