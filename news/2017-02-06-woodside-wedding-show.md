# 06 February 17 - Woodside wedding show

Perfect Snap attended the Wedding Fair held at the beautiful Wood house Hotel in Kenilworth.  
We have been to this location many times, however it was our first Wedding Fair of 2017!  
The Wood house Hotel is situated just on the outskirts of [Kenilworth in Warwickshire](/delivery-areas/#content). As you glide down the sweeping drive way visitors are able to admire the wonderful grounds that surround this outstanding venue. The most attractive building then awaits for your entrance.  
  
Team Perfect Snap arrived bright and early in order to set up the award winning [Green Screen Dream Machine Photobooth](/products/green-screen-photo-booths/#content) and the famous talking Magic Selfie Mirror Booth.  
This time we were situated in the suite which is at the rear of the hotel. A lovely bright large room where you can see everybody entering.  
  
The doors opened at 11.30 for the fair and we soon found visitors coming in to have a Selfie!  
  
Both our [Photo booth and Magic Selfie Mirror](/categories/photo-booths-and-magic-mirrors/#content) really do get some attention! There were a lot of prospective brides, grooms and their lovely families investigating our talking mirror' with the voice of Donkey from one of my favourite films Shrek! Remember, 'the mirror don't lie'!!. This always brings smiles to peoples faces! Many a photo were taken with our magnificent prop box also on offer which is always full of hats, glasses, signs and inflatable fun toys.

The Green Screen Dream Machine Photo booth also received many visitors. Guests at the Wedding Fair commented on what clear colourful pictures our booth produced and they were astounded at the amount of photo backgrounds that could be chosen. The magazine cover theme and the time machine seemed to be very popular along with the Big Eds feature!  
  
Before we knew it, the day had flown by! We had been so busy talking to all of the lovely visitors and hearing about their wedding plans as well as taking bookings it was time to say goodbye.  
Everyone loved our booth and talking mirror. Our January special offer of receiving 4 hours for the price of 3 was flying off the shelves, you really do like the 4 hour hire.

Due to popular demand we have extended our January offer 4 hours for the price of 3 in to February!!
