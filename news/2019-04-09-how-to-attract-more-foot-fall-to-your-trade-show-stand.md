## How To Attract More Foot Fall To Your Trade Show Stand

Arranging a new product launch in a car showroom or trade stand entertainment for an expo? Hiring interactive games will increase footfall & attract new clients for your business.

Walking around a busy Trade Show or Expo with a sea of stands trying to grab your attention isn't much fun, is it? It can be even worse if the exhibitors are staring at their phones and not engaging with passing potential clients. We know as we have been to many ourselves! Here are a few tips to remember when planning your stand:

**Planning Your Stand**

A worthy Trade Show Stand will have been planned and thought about well before the day. You need to think about:![busy expo stand](/userfiles/file/FunPro/busy-expo.jpg)

*   How you can tell your story about your brand
*   Ensure your name can be clearly seen
*   Make your visitors feel at home - be welcoming and friendly
*   Offer a memorable experience linked to your business

**Why Not Stand Out from The Norm?**

Instead of being like everyone else who does a competition to win a bottle of Champagne or gives away cans of pop, make your exhibition stand unique and you are sure to stand out from your competitors. By standing out from the crowd you will attract more footfall to your business and will have the opportunity to engage with more clients. Ultimately this will mean positive leads made on the day and once you follow up after the event it will earn you more revenue.

**Engagement Can Be Fun!**

You need your [Exhibition Stand](/category/exhibition-games/) entertainment to be engaging. Human beings are visual creatures - meaning if we like the look of something then we will want to know more about it. If a potential client sees something on your stand that they can resonate with they are more likely to stop by and speak with you for a few moments instead of walking on to your competitor next door.

Why not place a game on your stand that will catch the eyes of the attendees? By getting engagement and interaction with your new lead you are half way there - if they can have fun whilst at your expo stand then it will make your job of capturing that deal even easier. Competitions are a great way to obtain details but again you want a competition with a difference.

There will be many stands at your show who are marketing the same type of business as you. Having your company name or logo on a game hired from Fun Pro UK will help your potential client remember who you were and retain an image of your brand.

Everyone is so busy these days and has so much information thrown at them within such a short space of time it is all too easy to visualise a product but within minutes become side tracked and forget all about it. By branding a game on your stand this will guarantee you will be remembered and your new clients will know exactly who caught their eye at the expo they attended making it easier for them to contact you for future business.
