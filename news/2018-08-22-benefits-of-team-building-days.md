# [22 August 18 - Benefits of Team Building Days](/benefits-of-team-building-days/)

In every corporate business there is a variety of people interacting with one another on daily tasks and projects. The effectiveness of this communication will determine the success of the business. If colleagues can learn to work together through activities outside their usual tasks, there can be a better working relationship delivering cohesive results across departments.

There are a range of team building activities from problem solving to skills bases activities, to communicating with ice breakers. All contributing to the benefits of team building sessions.

Our top five benefits to these activities are:

1) Keeps employees motivated

Employees will take note that the business is taking an interest in their success and developing their skills. This is likely to be reciprocated by the employee as they will invest themselves in the business, through taking pride in their work and aiming to achieve company goals.

2) Improving communication

[Team building activities](/category/corporate-entertainment/) allow for clearer communication and co-operation between staff, which can develop into smoother relations between employees and success in projects.

3) Improved productivity

A range of team building activities will allow staff to see the skills of their colleagues and their approaches to problems. This knowledge is then transferred into the workplace, allowing individuals to benefit from each other's abilities within their work. Often allowing work to be completed in a more effective manner.

4) Developing problem solving skills

Lots of team building activities involves a group to solve a problem, under less pressure then they may feel within their office environment. This often results in employees reaching the solution, despite hurdles, and feeling more confident with their abilities. This new skill can be transferred back into the workplace.

5) Builds trust

These activities will enable employees to learn about each other through their interactions, allowing them to trust each other within and out of the corporate environment. A team without a level of trust in each other, simply won't function and won't achieve the company's goals.

There are both benefits to the individual and the company from undertaking team building activities.
