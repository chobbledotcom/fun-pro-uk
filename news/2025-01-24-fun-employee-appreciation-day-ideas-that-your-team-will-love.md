The world celebrates Employee Appreciation Day in March every year! Your team works hard, and they deserve a day that's dedicated to celebrating their efforts. Taking time to show appreciation creates a moment for connection and celebration; it’s a reminder that every contribution matters.

Many companies want to show appreciation to their staff, but might be working with a smaller budget. The good news is that you don’t need to spend a lot of money to make your team feel appreciated. A little creativity and thoughtfulness goes a long way.

A well planned celebration can make a big impact. It can boost employee morale, increase loyalty, and help build a stronger, more motivated workforce.

In this post, we will be covering some fun and exciting ideas you can use to celebrate your team and show them just how much you appreciate what they do.

### 2\. Personalised Notes or Gifts

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXemF28K4rCG86w6Jij5Vg42dwMFbl8q0LVSsCx8SgAoUSGjJJCJjlvXs3FGU8pWPQtoYMs178ImZLxN4M2GhKbaoM2k9btlsjzcnpFu_iAoG4RT1GORQH2LTdLXnGFGkW2FFeGCAw?key=3QGxajlrJekofxv54zo3rIjy)

Personalisation goes a long way. If words of affirmation are your way of showing appreciation, a personalised note or gifts are a good option. A lot of thought goes into handwriting notes and this naturally has a stronger effect on everyone.

Personalised notes or postcards are one of the best ways to show employees genuine gratitude and effort.

And when it comes to personalised gifts, employees appreciate something unique, something they want and appreciate. To up the ante and make things more interesting, you can encourage the whole team to give gifts to each other (even if it's not Christmas).

### 3\. Office Makeovers

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXewXYEX-rFLET7RRzraYXAboWb9FdD4oa_LVTId7z42QQn5PLD8soPPrmZZgBK3nsAzQhJ9_C2OhrgVM2MaCYmz0R8-T0iNRmc5gQoZedneypW9oD0M42uAp2L9eYTPuVqW59bp2g?key=3QGxajlrJekofxv54zo3rIjy)

Surprise your team with a workplace makeover. Refresh the office environment by introducing new décor, arranging comfortable break areas, or even adding fun elements like a photo booth or a snack bar.

A makeover will show your employees that you care about their work environment, and it makes them feel appreciated when they come to work.

### 4\. Wellness Activities and Retreats

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfjsYvDGnoviXe_c4CP5rMHAKDD7Lqunj7NQlKq_OYJFvGcI9uhz8UtjWNVUwGM_VcJiDg2MfDFJn6bGh5D7OSSx2n2OSct-zH_3okZ5o3ygZkBm1s4fg-hT5KsWKNwJ8-vLp_R?key=3QGxajlrJekofxv54zo3rIjy)

To increase job satisfaction and retention, employee wellbeing is important. Providing wellness activities on Employee Appreciation Day can show your team that you value their health and wellbeing.

Activities like yoga, meditation, a spa package, or even a healthy snack station can boost morale and help reduce workplace stress. But if your employees work remotely or live far from each other, you can buy packages that are close to their places or provide an allowance for a relaxing spa day.

### 5\. Employee Recognition and Awards Programmes

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeEcWdev1N5ee9e02OYVQP6fC9wjy64nVKVh-Mb4sAE9nKqZuu6JxyNlEJDvNFig5iu4yB-MVstX40lh9TTbck1EQM2RtOAb7VQCW6biGgA0VuRXDMKNRkFWNJcNPcHycJKQ5mVoA?key=3QGxajlrJekofxv54zo3rIjy)

You can boost employee morale with a consistent programme for [rewards and recognition](/events/company-award-ceremonies/#content). Of course, one day a year of appreciation is not enough to keep employee morale high, but it can help increase employee loyalty. You can create custom programmes using spreadsheets and gift card vendors.

Employee recognition platforms also exist where your team can do peer to peer recognitions, get rewards, and point gifting.

### 6\. Shoutouts on Employee Collaboration Platforms

Public appreciation should not be understated. Employee collaboration platforms like Skype, Microsoft Teams, Slack, etc can let you do this. Simply choose employees that you want to appreciate and write something for everyone to see. It might not take a lot of time, effort, or thought for you, but receiving public recognition can mean a lot for your team.

## Why Workers Appreciation Day Matters

If you're running a company and have a team of people working together with you, you have to remember that you're managing people. And you know what people need other than the basic resources? They need recognition, appreciation, and validation!

Recognising your employees on Employee Appreciation Day isn't just about one day of celebration, it's about creating a culture of appreciation that should be upheld all year round. When employees feel that their hard work is valued, it results in numerous benefits for both individuals and the company as a whole:

*   Increased Job Satisfaction: Regular recognition helps employees feel more satisfied in their roles, which leads to a more motivated and committed team.

*   Reduced Burnout: Recognising achievements and showing gratitude helps employees avoid burnout by reminding them of the value of their work.

*   Healthier Workplace Culture: Appreciation creates a positive, inclusive workplace culture where employees feel supported and motivated to do their best.

*   Higher Employee Retention: The happier your employees are, the more they would stay in their existing job and appreciate their own efforts at the company.

*   Strong Culture: Employee Appreciation Day is also a great way to promote a healthier work culture in your business. It makes everyone feel included and appreciated.

*   Better Productivity: If you appreciate employees, they will be motivated to perform well and some will even want to outdo their past achievements. Appreciation feels good and it inspires everyone to always be better.
