It was the first time we had attended the venue of Whittlebury Park in [Northamptonshire](/pages/nottingham-corporate-event-hire/) and I really must say it was stunning!  
The Perfect Snap team arrived in plenty of time as normal to set our [Magic Mirror](/category/photo-booths-and-magic-mirrors/magic-mirror/) and overflowing box of props up ready to begin the evening of fun entertainment!  
We always enjoy attending wedding receptions, all the guests have  
had such a brilliant day and once the fun begins with their friends our team get busy helping them out taking that fabulous selfie!

Perfect Snap had a very busy evening with a lot of happy guests at Whittlebury Park and would like to wish the new bride and groom a very long and happy future together!
