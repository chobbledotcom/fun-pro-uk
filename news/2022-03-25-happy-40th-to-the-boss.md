# [25 March 22 - Happy 40th to the Boss!!](/happy-40th-to-the-boss/)

Happy 40th to the Boss!!

When we received a last minute call asking how we can help with a 40th birthday celebration at a work place in Nuneaton, Warwickshire yesterday we quickly put our thinking caps on!

It was all a big surprise, the staff were arranging a celebration for the boss at their work place.

How exciting!

The Fun Pro Team jumped into action thinking what we had available at such short notice and which game from the huge variety of fun games we stock, would be best for everyone to enjoy.

Our team didn't take long before they came up with the ultimate fantastic game,... Bully!

Bully as he is lovingly known is Fun Pro's friendly [rodeo bull](/category/fun-days/46/rodeo-bull/). He loves to come out for parties and events, as long as he has been fed and watered by the team he will perform for the crowd with his twists and turns ensuring not many people stay on his back for long!

When we came up with the idea for the party our clients were in awe as they were struggling to think of a special game they could hire for their do!

The Fun Pro delivery team set Bully up in pride of place, he was ready for the festivities to begin! The birthday boy was the first contestant to see how long he could hang on - all on his 40th birthday too! Bully was in a good mood that day, the twisting and turning began, the crowd gathered to see how long their boss could possibly hang on for.

As the seconds went by it was all looking so good, maybe Bully decided to have the last laugh with the birthday boy? Before we knew it Bully was riding solo, we could hear a little tune with the lyrics ' I'm gonna take my horse to the old town road, I'm gonna ride til i can't no more!' Maybe we have a singing bull as well??

The laughter subsided and the boss did a great job but i don't think he will become a cowboy anytime soon!

Each staff member had a go at keeping Bully under control, it was a great birthday celebration that all staff members took part in and loved every minute especially the boss!

Happy 40th Birthday from the Fun Pro Team!!
