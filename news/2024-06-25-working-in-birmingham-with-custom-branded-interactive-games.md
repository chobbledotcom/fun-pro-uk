At Fun Pro UK, we recently had the exciting opportunity to showcase our expertise in enhancing trade shows by installing seven custom-branded [interactive games](/categories/interactive-game-hire/#content) for our clients at the Installer show held at the [NEC Birmingham](/locations/birmingham/nec/#content). Our close proximity in Wolston allowed us to offer complimentary delivery, ensuring that each setup was convenient and cost-effective for our clients.

Each of the games provided was carefully branded with the client's logo and key messaging, turning each unit into a pivotal marketing tool that complemented the exhibitors' stands beautifully. Our in-house design team meticulously ensured that the branding was spot-on, enhancing visual appeal and brand presence on the show floor.

The variety of games we set up for the event included:

1.  **[The Vault - Honeywell](/products/the-vault/#content)**: Engaged visitors with a high-stakes challenge of cracking the code against the clock.
2.  **[Ballnado - Spirotech UK & Anton by Crowcon](/products/ballnado-grabber/#content)**: Drew crowds with an energetic and visually engaging game where participants scrambled to grab flying balls.
3.  **[Mega Wire - @A-Gas](/products/mega-wire-branded-game-hire-stand-alone/#content)**: Offered a steady hand challenge as attendees navigated a wand along a twisting wire.
4.  **[Whack a Mole - @LG Electronics and VIVA Sanitary](/products/whack-a-mole-game-hire/#content)**: Provided fast-paced fun as participants tried to whack as many 'moles' as possible.
5.  **[Beat the Buzzer - Correla](/products/beat-the-buzzer/#content)**: Tested participants' precision and steadiness in a tense buzzer game.

With the Installer show running over several days, we are excited to see how our [exhibition stand games](/categories/exhibition-games/#content) enhance the experience for our clients' stands, hoping to boost footfall and engagement significantly. Our aim is for these [bespoke branded games](/categories/branded-exhibition-games/#content) to attract attendees and turn visitors into profit.
