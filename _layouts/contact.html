How It Works

  1. You Book

    Tell us your event date, venue, and what you need. We'll confirm availability and pricing.

  2. We Plan

    Our team works out logistics, confirms access requirements, and schedules your delivery slot.

  3. We Setup

    On the day, we turn up early with everything you've booked. Setup, testing, ready to go.

  4. We Remove

    When it's over, we pack down and disappear. You won't even know we were there.
